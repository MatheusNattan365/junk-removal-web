We donate and recycle first—landfill last.

  • Dispose: Only what can't be reused or recycled

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?
We proudly serve Hamilton, Burlington, Oakville, Milton, Mississauga, and surrounding areas. Not sure if we cover your location? Just ask!
How does pricing work?
Our pricing is based on volume—the space your items take up in our trailer. We provide an upfront quote before we start, so there are no surprises. Labor, loading, cleanup, and disposal are all included.
Do you provide same-day pickup?
Yes! Same-day and next-day service is often available depending on our schedule. Contact us to check availability.
What items can't you take?
We cannot take hazardous materials (chemicals, paint, asbestos), medical waste, or certain regulated items. If you're unsure, just ask—we're happy to help find a solution.
Do you donate or recycle?
We donate and recycle whenever possible. Usable items go to local charities, and recyclables are taken to proper facilities. The landfill is always our last resort.
Do I need to move items outside?
Nope! We're full-service—we'll come inside, carry items out, and clean up after. Just point to what goes, and we'll handle the rest.
Are you insured?
Yes, we are fully licensed and insured for your peace of mind.
How do I book?
Easy! Fill out the contact form below, give us a call, or send us an email. We'll respond quickly with availability and a quote.
